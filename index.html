Stage I


We wrote the web crawlers and parsers to collect data on YELP and TripAdvisor.

The data includs roughly 3000 restaurants from Los Angeles. We collected the fundatmental information, such as telephone number, address, zip code, and ratings. We aslo crawled ~300 text files on the short reviews of the restaurant from Yelp for future text extraction.

Stage II


In this stage, we extracted the resturant name from 313 text files.

We marked the restaurant name as positive entities with mark as "{}", and chose the close number of other words as negative entities with mark as "[]". After seperating the 313 text files to two sets, we applied machine learning of 5 different methods on the training set with ~200 text files, and test the performance of our classifier on the test set with ~100 text files.
